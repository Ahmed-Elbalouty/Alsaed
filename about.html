About Us

Our Story

AlSaed Alsharqi Companys (AAC) story began over 24 years ago, in the year 2000, from a small office in Al Dammam, Saudi Arabia, as a local establishment focused on small construction projects. At that time, it was known as Fadaa AlSharqia Group for Contracting. Over the years, AAC has taken huge leaps forward to become a top-tier Grade A general contracting company, offering a full spectrum of integrated and turnkey engineering, along with high-level construction services. Our company boasts strong financial stability to support large-scale projects, and we offer a wide range of professional services. Our highly skilled team specializes in :

  • Construction & Maintenance
  • Project Management
  • Electrical, Mechanical, and Electromechanical Works
